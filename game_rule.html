Welcome to Breakout

Game Rules

Objective: Breakout is a classic arcade game where the player controls a paddle to bounce a ball and break bricks. The objective is to clear all the bricks from the screen.

Controls

  • Use the left and right arrow keys or mouse/touch to move the paddle.
  • Launch the ball by pressing the spacebar or clicking/tapping on the screen.

Game Elements

  • Paddle: The player-controlled element used to bounce the ball.
  • Ball: Bounces off the paddle and breaks bricks.
  • Bricks: Target objects to be destroyed by the ball.

Scoring

Earn points for each brick destroyed. Some bricks may require multiple hits to break.

Levels

The game may have multiple levels with varying brick arrangements and challenges. Complete each level by clearing all the bricks.

Game Over

The game ends if the ball falls below the paddle. You may have limited lives or chances to complete the game.

Tips

  • Try to keep the ball in play by bouncing it off the paddle.
  • Avoid missing the ball as it reduces your chances of completing the level.
  • Some bricks may provide power-ups or extra points when destroyed.
